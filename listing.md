### Should you rent or buy? Get the numbers.

The ClearMetric Rent vs Buy Calculator is a **professional Excel spreadsheet** that helps you decide whether renting or buying a home is better financially — for your situation, your market, and your timeframe.

### What's Inside

**Sheet 1: Rent vs Buy Calculator**
- All inputs in one place with teal input cells (easy to spot)
- Home purchase price, down payment, mortgage rate, term
- Monthly rent, annual rent increase
- Property tax, insurance, HOA, maintenance, closing costs
- Home appreciation, investment return, tax bracket
- Instant results: monthly payment, break-even year, verdict
- Monthly cost comparison (buy vs rent)

**Sheet 2: Year-by-Year Comparison**
- 30 rows of detailed projection
- Home value, mortgage balance, buyer equity
- Cumulative cost of buying vs renting
- Renter investments (down payment + opportunity cost)
- Clear "Buy wins" / "Rent wins" by year
- Line chart: Buyer equity vs Renter investments over time
- Summary verdict at a glance

- Compare 3 scenarios side-by-side
- Current Market: uses your main inputs
- Optimistic: lower rates, higher appreciation, higher investment returns
- Conservative: higher rates, lower appreciation, lower investment returns
- See which scenario favors buying vs renting

**Sheet 4: How To Use**
- Complete guide to every input
- How to interpret break-even and verdict
- What-if scenario tips
- Important notes (PMI, tax assumptions, limitations)
